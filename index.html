    Hello world! I'm Anthony and I'm a developer!

    Anthony Cho

    Rice University Computer Science Major

    I was born in small city in South Korea called Pohang. However, I was raised in Houston, TX where I've pursued a variety of interests ranging from gardening to breakdancing. My greatest interest, however, has always been in creating. I have developed CAD designs, a satellite callibration program, android applications, websites, circuits, robots, and the like.

    I'm also passionate about entrepreneurship. I've won $10,000 from the LILIE New Entrepreneurs Grant and $1000 from Rice's Annual Undergraduate Elevator Pitch Competition for my designs/ideas. I'm very involved in the Rice entrepreneurship community and am always looking to participate in events such as the Houston 3 Day Start Up.

    I like to create products with a strong value proposition but I also recognize the value of creating for the betterment of society as a whole. I believe that developers have a powerful ability to impact this world in an increasing number of diverse and positive ways. In high school I held multiple officer positions, such as President of Earth Club, for service organizations. Additionally, I was director of the Student Heroes Program where I oversaw hundreds of student representatives from several dozen different high schools around Houston at the largest food bank in the nation as a part of a volunteer leadership program. As director I also founded the Board of Liaisons which pushed for initiatives such as Students End Hunger: an event that ran food drives in over three dozen schools.
